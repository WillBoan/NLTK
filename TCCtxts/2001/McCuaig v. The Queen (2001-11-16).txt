Reasonsfor Judgment

Beaubier, J.T.C.C.

[1] The applications were called for hearing at Victoria, British Columbia on November 9, 2001. The Applicant did not appear at the hearing. The Respondent's counsel did appear.

[2] The Respondent's counsel filed a motion in each file to dismiss the Applicant's applications to extend the time for filing Notices of Objection and to extend the time for filing Notices of Appeal. Each application was accompanied by an affidavit of Barry Fong, which described in detail the factual basis upon which the Applicant's applications should be dismissed. There is no evidence which contradicts Barry Fong's affidavits.

[3] For these reasons, the Applicant's applications are dismissed.

Signed at Ottawa, Canada, this 16th day of November, 2001.

"D. W. Beaubier"
